## Configure Mp3Tag

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and extract the config" stepNumber={1}>
    [Download](https://github.com/LLRIX-ofc/docs/raw/refs/heads/main/Mp3tagSettings.zip) the configuration and then extract the zip
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Mp3Tag" stepNumber={2}>
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open configuration folder">
    Press on `File`, then press on `Open configuration folder`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the config">
    Open the folder you downloaded and press `ctrl + a` then right click and press copy
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close Mp3Tag">
    Make sure the app is fully closed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the configuration">
    Go to the `Mp3tag` folder then right click and press paste
  </Step>
  <Step title="Replace">
    If you get a pop-up asking `Replace or Skip Files,` press `Replace the files in the destination`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Congrats">
    Now your Mp3Tag is ready to use for apple music local files
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Get your music files ready

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new folder">
    Name it something that fits like "local songs" or "local music
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add you songs">
    Add all of your songs into the folder
  </Step>
  <Step title="Convert them to m4a">
    [Downlaod](https://github.com/LLRIX-ofc/docs/raw/refs/heads/main/app.py) the python script, place it in your music folder, and then double click to run it

    > This script converts all your music files to m4a which is needed for full funcitonality in apple music
  </Step>
  <Step title="Congrats">
    Now your files are ready for the next step
  </Step>
</Steps>
